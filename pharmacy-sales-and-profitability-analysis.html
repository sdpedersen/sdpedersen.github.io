Pharmacy Sales & Profitability Analysis

Project Overview

This project analyzes pharmacy sales data to identify key drivers of revenue and profitability. The goal was to uncover trends in product performance, store efficiency, and customer behavior.

Tools Used

  • Power BI
  • Power Query

Key Insights

  • Top-performing product categories contributed the majority of revenue.
  • Certain stores consistently underperformed due to low transaction volume.
  • Seasonal trends significantly impacted sales.
