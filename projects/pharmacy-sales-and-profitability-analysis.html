Pharmacy Sales & Profitability Analysis

Overview

  • This project analyzes sales and profitability performance across a European pharmaceutical distribution network serving 120 pharmacy customers.
  • The goal was to uncover geographic and product-level performance patterns that influence revenue growth and profitability stability.
  • A multi-page Power BI dashboard was developed to evaluate customer performance, product portfolio dynamics, and promotional impact on margins.
  • The analysis focuses on identifying structural performance gaps, growth opportunities, and areas requiring targeted operational support.

Key Insights

  • Urban pharmacies consistently outperform regional benchmarks, while suburban and rural locations remain below average despite year-over-year improvements.
  • Prescription products generate the largest share of revenue but operate with structurally lower margins, highlighting the need for disciplined pricing and promotion strategies.
  • Wellness and Personal Care categories provide higher profitability potential, reinforcing their importance as margin-supporting segments.
  • Most Medical Devices products remain concentrated in low-volume, low-profitability segments, indicating opportunities for portfolio reassessment.
  • Promotional activity reduces margins across all product categories, with the largest margin impacts observed in Prescription products.

Tools Used

  • Power Query for data profiling and quality validation
  • Power BI for data visualization
